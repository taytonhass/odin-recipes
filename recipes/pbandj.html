Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Smooth or chunky peanut butter
  • Jelly of your choice of flavor

Steps

  1. Place two pieces of bread on a plate side-by-side.
  2. Use a butter knife to spread peanut butter on one side of one of the slices of bread.
  3. Use a clean knife to spread the jelly onto one side of the other slice of bread.
  4. Place the side of bread with peanut butter on it on the side of bread with jelly on it so that the two sides are touching.
  5. Eat the finished product.
