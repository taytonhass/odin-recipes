How to Make Kraft Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • A Box of Kraft Macaroni and Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter (Optional)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill a pot halfway full with clean water and place it on the stove until it boils.
  2. Open the box of Kraft Macaroni and Cheese and place the noodles into the water.
  3. Stir the noodles occasionally while letting them boil for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Strain the noodles of water.
  5. Mix a splash of milk (and optionally 2tbsp of butter) into the noodles.
  6. Open the cheese packet and pour contents into the noodles and stir.
  7. Once everything is combined, scoop noodles into a bowl and eat.
