How to Make a Bowl of Frosted Flakes

Ingredients

  • Frosted Flakes
  • Milk or a Milk Alternative

Steps

  1. Place a bowl and spoon on the counter.
  2. Pour the bag of Frosted Flakes into the bowl until the flakes reach nearly the top of the bowl.
  3. Pour milk into the bowl containing the Frosted Flakes until you reach your desired amount of milk.
  4. Eat the cereal and milk with a spoon and enjoy.
